Discover our most famous Rides

RapidRush Adventures - Rogue River, Oregon

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting amidst the stunning scenery of the Rogue River. Navigate exciting rapids, surrounded by lush forests and wildlife, creating unforgettable memories for adventurers of all levels.

Cascade Currents Expeditions - Salmon River, Idaho

Embark on an adrenaline-fueled journey with Cascade Currents on the Salmon River. Enjoy challenging rapids, breathtaking canyons, and a true wilderness escape, making it the ultimate rafting destination.

BlueWave Rafting - New River Gorge, West Virginia

Dive into the heart of Appalachia with BlueWave Rafting on the New River Gorge. Brave exhilarating rapids while soaking in the rugged beauty of the gorge, making it an ideal destination for thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts alike.
